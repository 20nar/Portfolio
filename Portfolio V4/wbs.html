Website Mockup

Logo

The homepage shows instantly who the Nachtwakers, with very natural shot with all the 4 band members. This is a one-pager website design. The user can scroll down without really having to go to another page. The navigation bar will be a sticky-top, therefore the user can just click on certain elements and the page automatically scrolls down to that section. The user has the choice to just scroll or to quickly click on one of those headers, which bring them to the desired section.


About us

I wanted to create this "About Us" section, with the individual members, to tell a little bit more about them. I wanted to illustrate it as if they were "wanted". This turned out to be quiet interesting. With their faces behind the text, just enough to make out who it is, but not too much that it disturbs the text.
