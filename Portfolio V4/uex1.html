Sprint 0

Target group research

The ‘Nachwakers’ are a ska reggae band and only preform at night. They are quite new and we have never seen or heard of them before.

In this stage it is imperative to go to one of their live concerts. That is, if they do have live performances. While we listen to the band, we also gather information from the people attending this performance or concert.

This could be done with a simple interview. After gathering information from the listeners, we would also interview the band so we could put these questions side by and show the band the information that we gathered.

Is this their first time listening to this band? What do the listener think of this band? What do they feel while they listen to this band? Who does the band sound like? Do they sound like another band that they know?

    Conclusion

    It becomes apparent that some people think that the Nachtwakers sounds a lot like Doe Maar. The same vibe, the same intro almost the same voice.

    This led us to believe that they want to be assosciated with the colors of Doe Maar, but Kevin really did not want that at all. He wanted a more minimalistic black and white logo.

    Interviewing these people made us realize that every young and old would really like this music. But of course in order to gain some popularity we would have to target the younger audience, since they use Spotify and YouTube a lot more. In order for us to really brand the Nachtwakers, we thought it would be best to change the logo, give it a bit more ska/reggae and festival vibe.

    And finally we also concluded that most people would say that this music would be played at festivals. So that would be another idea for the Nachtwakers to take into consideration. People of all ages would enjoy this music, whether its reminiscing the 80's Doe Maar or a nice festival song they would enjoy.

    Usability test

    This is the conclusion of the usability tests that were performed on 6 users. The outcome went very well, but there were a few things that more than 1 person noticed, these are described below.

    Atmosphere / style

    Users find the website to come across as semi-serious, a bit more serious than unserious. Informal use of language, but serious formal style of the website. Users find it professional. The band seems to be serious about their music career.

    The users find the website to be fair and open. Users think the band is reliable.

    The users think the website appears gentle.

    Users find the website clean and minimalistic. Issues Users could not find the music section good. Users cannot find out which music the band is playing.

    The users find the Ska / Reggae part unnecessary.
