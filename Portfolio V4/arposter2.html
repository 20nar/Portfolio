Augmented Reality Poster V2

In this version I brought the poster much close to the character . The tour dates are also present in the back of the poster. The character looks as if it is right on the poster.


Idea

After getting great feedback from Kevin I added all the four charachters. I imported a stage and replaced the image marker with the new Poster that Bahar made. It still works exactly like the previous A.R Poster. The user points the camera at the poster and the animation starts.

Execution The user is instantly brought into a live concert of the Nachtwakers. This does not show the tour dates yet in the animation, it is only visible in the poster itself. Because it just a simple animation that loops.

Final A.R Poster

After realizing that the 3D Model characters were too big to import into the lens, I decided to make the animation 2D.

It has the same concept as the other lenses, but in this one I put a Snapcode tracking at the bottom so anyone can scan the code and download the lens from the Snapchat Lens Library. Bahar was the one that made animation and I put the animation on top of the image tracking.

This lens is now available on Snapchat right now! Scan the snapchat code on the right to get the lens!

Conclusion

It was quite an experience to try to understand how the Lens Studio program works. It is very similar to Cinema 4D, the controls are pretty identical. But of course you can do way more with Cinemas 4D. The only problem we encountered was that the lens cannot exceed the 4MB limit.
