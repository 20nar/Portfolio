Shot and edited with:

DJI Osmo 2 & Samsung A7

In order for me to keep up with the skateboarder I used a stabilizer to always keep the camera steady.Using the wide angle lens of the Samsung a7 I was able to get the skateboarder fully in the shot every time.

Canon EOS 650D

In this video clip I also wanted to get some shots of the city and some close up shots of the skateboarders. I used the standard Canon EF-S 18-55mm F/3.5-5.6 II occasion lens.

Adobe Premiere 2020

This was my fist time actually using Adobe Premiere. My previous work I usualy did in Filmora Pro. Premiere is so much easier and there so many different things you can use. .
