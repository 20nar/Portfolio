Logo for Sprint 2

Old Logo

After the presentation of Sprint 1 for the Nachtwakers, Kevin did not like the logo that I designed. He said that it looks a bit like a restaurant logo. That is why I redesigned the logo. Kevin really wants to keep their own logo, but I gave it one last show to change it.


Logo V1

After the presentation of Sprint 1 I showed Kevin some of my logos and he really liked these two. So then I just made some itterations on those. Added the colors.


Logo V2

In these itterations I wanted to put the letter N and W together. And of course add the colors with it as well. But the first one seems to be too hectic and the second one looks like a "On Air" sign from a radio station.


Logo V3

With this logo I tried to put make this negative space between the N and W. Therefore it still looks like an N that connects with the W. This does not really give the Nachtwakers vibe and it again looks like a "On Air" sign.


Final Logo V4

I went back to the first design of Sprint 1. But I removed the Moon and cityscape of Tilburg. That left me with just the font and that little star in the middle of the A. This still gives a night feel to the logo. Simple, clean and uniform.

I also made the logo responsive. When the size of the logo gets adjusted on a website, it will respond by splitting Nachtwakers in two and putting it underneath each other.
