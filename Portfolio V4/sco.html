Build a B(r)and

Project Description

For a starting band or artist (act) it is quite difficult to make a name in the music industry. To be successful in this industry an exceptional musical talent is not enough.In a group context, we worked on an assignment named "Build a B (r) and). For a client, an artist or a band, we worked on the brand, the identity of the band and the user experience that will result in associated digital products. Our process is based on iterations.

Sprint 0

In this first sprint we first had to understand who the Nachtwakers are. We created some Artistic Explorations and Brandguide Inspirations to fully understand the band.

The nachtwakers is a Ska/Regga band from Tilburg. They're in their final years of the Fontys Rock Academy. Like the name implies, they always play and make music at night.

I placed some images together that fit best with the band.

We searched for images on the following keywords

  • Arsitc Exploration
  • Target Audience Research
