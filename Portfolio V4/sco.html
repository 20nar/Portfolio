Build a B(r)and

Project Description

For a starting band or artist (act) it is quite difficult to make a name in the music industry. To be successful in this industry an exceptional musical talent is not enough.In a group context, we worked on an assignment named "Build a B (r) and). For a client, an artist or a band, we worked on the brand, the identity of the band and the user experience that will result in associated digital products. Our process is based on iterations.

Sprint 0

In this first sprint we first had to understand who the Nachtwakers are. We created some Artistic Explorations and Brandguide Inspirations to fully understand the band.

The nachtwakers is a Ska/Regga band from Tilburg. They're in their final years of the Fontys Rock Academy. Like the name implies, they always play and make music at night.

I placed some images together that fit best with the band.

Artistic Exploration Research

Sprint 1

In this second sprint we started with some brandguide inspiration and a complete brandguide. Next to that we also created a mock-up for the website. The website also had to be tested with real people. Because of this we made a usability test.

Brandguide Website Mock-up Research

Sprint 2

In this sprint we made posters that we would use for the A.R posters.

We also wanted to create A.R posters with 3D models, but we left that for a different product

The posters were also tested with multiple interview test

In this step we also started with the customer journey, so we could understand how these products would actually work in the real world

A.R Poster Poster Research Logo

Sprint 3

This is the last sprint. We finished our website and A.R Posters. Next to that we also made a Social Media Campagne

In this sprint we really started to work with the 3D Models to make a backdrop concept and coaster concept

At the end we polished everything to finish all the design and concepts. Therefore making all the products relate to one another and have the same colors and design rules that we placed in the brandguide.
