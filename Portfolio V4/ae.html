Artistic Exploration

In order to understand the nachtwakers we made some moodboards with images that contain certain elements from the band.

This moodboard contains the nightlife feel, colorful, reggae, ska, bob marley and other media design trends that I am interested in.

Execution

The nachtwakers is a Ska/Regga band from Tilburg. They're in their final years of the Fontys Rock Academy. Like the name implies, they always play and make music at night. After seeing the logo and the pictures of the band we realized it was quite minimalistic and black and white. Espescially when you look at the logo and the clothes they were wearing. The music sounds like it could be played at a festival. The pop/reggae vibes are very apparent in the way they sound. This would definetely be great at a festival.

Reflection

We compared all of the moodboard of the group and grouped them in three different moodboards that we think fits best with the Nachtwakers.

  • Minimalistic Black and White
  • Festival
  • Neon lights and Night life

    Ska and Reggae

    The Nachtwakers are clearly a ska and reggae band.I combined all these images of different artist that sounds quite similar to the Nachtwakers.

    Doe Maar is an obvious comparison when it comes to the Nachtwakers, but the way they present themselves is quite different from the bright colors from Doe Maar.

    Ska usually has this checkered black and white design, that gives it this goofy but yet minimalistic feel.

    Bob Marley

    The greatest reggae artist influencer ever lived has changed so many lives and resonates within each and every one of us, even in 2019 you hear Bob Marleys music.

    When we conducted the first interview with Kevin, he told us that Bob Marley was one of his influencers. Kevin also mentioned that he wants to spread the same peace and joy, just like how Bob Marley's music did.

    Ska usually has this checkered black and white design, that gives it this goofy but yet minimalistic feel.

    Festival and live band

    Whenever I hear the song "Alles' from the Nachtwakers, it really gives me a festival vibe. Preforming live infront a big crowd would suite every band of course. But as we've seen in the video how they interact with the crowd, the Nachtwakers would really pump up the crowd. Therefore I searched some images that show these aspects.

    Own Inspiration

    A lot of my inspiration comes from the bright night colors of the Caribbean. I've always been interested in psychedelic visuals. It is very pleasing to look at. The blue, pink and red velvet colors really inspire me to create more visuals in the future.

    Moon and "semi-serious"

    The Nachtwakers have a moon in their logo, that is the reason why I searched for pictures of the moon to get some inspiration.

    Kevin from the Nachtwakers said in the first interview that he sees the band as semi-serious. That is why I have images of skeletons. But they are either smiling or in a semi-serious setting.

    Digital Trends

    There are many different digital trends and I looked up some trends that I thought were quite intersting.

    I love the simplicity in some pages. And also the complexcity of other pages.

    Conclusion

    After doing some exstensive Artistic Exploration, I found that the Nachtwakers don't really fit with all the reggae/ska bands. They did not want to be associated with the colors of reggae. But by doing this it made me realize that they don't have to use those colors, because they aren't really a reggae/ska band. Doe maar did not use those colors at all.
